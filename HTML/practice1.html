Nelson Mandela

The Mandela Effect


Nelson Mandela was a Social Activist in South Africa. He was able to stop the racial division happening in South Africa. Since the 1940s, he participated in peaceful protests and armed restistances. This will lead to him being put in jail not being release until 1990. After that in 1994 he ran and became the first black president of South Africa. Here he uses his power to transform the country into a multiethnic place. When he retire from politics in 1999 he left an impact as a activist for peace and social justice until he died in 2013.
